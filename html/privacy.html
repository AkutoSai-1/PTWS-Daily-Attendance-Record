Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Princes Town West Secondary School values the privacy of our staff and students. This Privacy Policy outlines how our Roll Call application handles information on your device.

1. No Server-Side Data Collection

This web application functions entirely on your local web browser. We do not maintain any external databases or server-side systems. Student names, surnames, dates, and attendance markings are never uploaded to the internet or transmitted anywhere outside your computer.

2. Use of Local Browser Cookies

We use temporary client-side browser cookies solely for functional utility purposes—specifically, to save your current progress on the attendance grid so you do not lose your work if you close or refresh your browser tab during the day.

3. Automated Data Deletion

To maximize data security and ensure that student identity markers are never permanently logged, all cookies generated by this site are set to hard-expire and permanently erase themselves from your browser memory every single day at exactly 2:45 PM.

4. Managing Your Privacy

Because all records live directly within your browser, you can clear all student names and attendance markers instantly at any point by manually clearing your browser cache/cookies or by spam-clicking the table's delete action items.
